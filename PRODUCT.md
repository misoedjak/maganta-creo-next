## Users
- Primary: Corporate marketing directors, event organizers (EOs), and BUMN public relations leads. They require high-reliability event fabrication (booths, stages) with clean finishes and structural calculations.
- Context: They need trustworthy fabricators who can deliver under tight load-in timelines (usually 24–48 hours) without defects or safety issues.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a premium digital showcase and lead generation platform for Maganta Kreasi event fabrication services in Indonesia.

## Positioning
Maganta Kreasi is the premier event fabrication partner in Indonesia, combining rigorous structural engineering calculations with flawless aesthetic craftsmanship.

## Conversion & proof
- Primary CTA: WhatsApp direct chat for immediate negotiation.
- Secondary CTA: Detailed digital "Request Quote" form submitting parameters into the leads database.
- One line to remember: "Precision Engineering, Exceptional Craftsmanship."
- Belief Ladder:
  1. Maganta Kreasi owns a massive in-house workshop, not outsourcing fabrication.
  2. Every double-deck structure and large stage is load-calculated for absolute safety.
  3. They finish setups hours before showtime to guarantee peace of mind.
- Proof: Client list including BUMNs and major Indonesian banks (Bank Mandiri, BCA, Pertamina, Telkomsel) and past featured showcase projects.
